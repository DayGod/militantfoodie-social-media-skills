# Skill: Content Matrix

> Generate 30+ post ideas instantly, mapped by content type, platform, and theme — in Mike's voice.

---

## How Claude Uses This Skill

When Mike asks for a content matrix, Claude generates a structured grid of post ideas across multiple content types. Every idea should feel specific, not generic — rooted in Mike's voice, themes, and seasonal context. A good idea should make Mike think *"I want to make that"* not *"sure, I guess I could."*

---

## Instructions

1. **Ask for context if not provided:** season or month, any upcoming events, dishes Mike is working on, or a general theme (e.g. summer produce, comfort food, Italian classics).
2. **Generate ideas across these content types:**
   - Recipe reveal
   - Ingredient spotlight
   - Technique/tip
   - Story-driven (nostalgia, travel, market find)
   - Opinion/hot take
   - Engagement bait (question, poll, debate)
   - Behind the scenes
   - Repurpose (IG → Substack bridge)
3. **Map each idea to:** platform (IG, Substack, or both), content type, and a one-line hook.
4. **Aim for 30–40 ideas.** Quantity matters here — Mike picks what resonates.
5. **Voice check:** every idea should be rooted in Mike's themes (Sunday dinners, markets, seasons, chef culture). Nothing generic.

---

## Output Format

Present as a markdown table:

| # | Idea | Type | Platform | Hook |
|---|------|------|----------|------|
| 1 | Cacio e pepe in July — wrong season, right craving | Recipe reveal | IG + Substack | *"Some dishes don't care what month it is."* |

---

## Example Prompt

> *"Build me a content matrix for [month/season]. I'm currently working on [dish or theme]. Give me 30+ ideas across all content types with a hook for each."*

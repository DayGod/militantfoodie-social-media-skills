# Version History — Militant Foodie AI Content System

## v1.0 — 2026-04-29

### 🚀 Initial Release

First complete version of the Militant Foodie AI Content System. Includes the Voice Profile foundation and a full library of 14 skills across writing, formatting, planning, and visuals.

---

### Added

#### Foundation
- **`skills/voice-profile.md`** — Core voice document. Defines Mike's personality, audience, storytelling themes, platform voice (Instagram vs. Substack), engagement moves, Do/Don't rules, and example captions. The required brief for every content session.

#### Writing Skills
- **`skills/post-writer.md`** — Full Instagram caption writer. Hook, story, CTA. Outputs two versions per brief: full story and punchy.
- **`skills/hook-generator.md`** — Generates 5–10 hook variations across 7 styles (curiosity gap, bold statement, sensory, nostalgic, contradiction, one-word, time anchor).
- **`skills/newsletter-voice.md`** — Full Substack newsletter writer. Subject line, preview text, opener, body, recipe bridge, recipe, and sign-off.
- **`skills/reel-scripting.md`** — Scene-by-scene reel scripts with shot descriptions, voiceover, text overlays, timing, and audio direction.
- **`skills/pinned-comment.md`** — Three pinned comment options per post across three angles: recipe tease, Substack bridge, engagement starter.

#### Formatting & Quality Skills
- **`skills/post-formatter.md`** — Formats captions for Instagram. Line breaks, CTA placement, hashtag strategy, voice check.
- **`skills/post-scorer.md`** — Scores captions across 5 criteria (hook, voice, story, CTA, formatting) out of 50. Includes improvement notes and a post/needs work/rewrite verdict.

#### Planning Skills
- **`skills/content-matrix.md`** — Generates 30+ post ideas mapped by content type, platform, and hook from a theme or season brief.
- **`skills/niche-research.md`** — Web-search powered trend research filtered through Mike's voice and niche. Outputs Act On Now / Watch / Skip tiers with specific post angles.
- **`skills/analytics-dashboard.md`** — Interprets pasted Instagram and Substack performance data. Identifies patterns, what to double down on, and produces 5 data-backed post recommendations.

#### Visual Skills
- **`skills/graphic-designer.md`** — Creative briefs for any visual asset — composition, lighting, palette, mood, text overlay direction.
- **`skills/gemini-carousel.md`** — Full carousel briefs: slide copy, visual direction per slide, and ready-to-paste Gemini image prompts.
- **`skills/gemini-infographic.md`** — Infographic copy, layout direction, and Gemini image prompts for techniques, comparisons, and tip lists.

#### Repo
- **`README.md`** — Full system documentation. Quick start, skill library, four typical workflows, and all raw URLs.
- **`VERSION.md`** — This file.

```

```

**Version numbering guide:**
- `v1.0, v2.0` — Major release (new system, significant rebuild)
- `v1.1, v1.2` — Minor release (new skill added, workflow updated)
- `v1.0.1, v1.0.2` — Patch (small fix or copy edit to an existing skill)
